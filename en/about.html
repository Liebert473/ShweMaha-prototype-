About Shwe Maha

At Shwe Maha, we take pride in being a leading rubber trading and production company in Kachin State, Myanmar. With years of experience in the natural rubber industry, we specialize in Ribbed Smoked Sheets (RSS) and are committed to delivering high-quality rubber that meets industry standards.

Our journey began with a simple yet strong mission â€“ to connect local rubber producers with businesses in need of high-grade rubber while ensuring fair trade and sustainable practices. Over the years, we have built a strong reputation for reliability, transparency, and quality, making us a preferred choice in the region.

Company History
2015

The Beginning

Shwe Maha was founded with a vision to support local rubber farmers and provide high-quality natural rubber to businesses. Starting as a small-scale trading business, we focused on building relationships with suppliers and understanding industry needs.

2018

Expansion & Growth

With increasing demand for Ribbed Smoked Sheets (RSS), we expanded our operations, improving processing techniques and implementing quality control measures to meet higher industry standards. Our commitment to reliability helped us gain recognition in the market.

2021

Strengthening Our Network

We built strong partnerships with suppliers and customers, establishing Shwe Maha as a trusted name in Kachin State. Our focus on sustainability and ethical trading practices allowed us to contribute to the growth of the local rubber industry.

2024

Aiming for the Future

Today, Shwe Maha continues to grow with a mission to expand our reach, improve production efficiency, and maintain high-quality standards. We are actively seeking new customers and suppliers, ensuring that we remain a key player in the regional rubber market.
